# Welcome to LAOM

LAOM is much more than just a place to live. It's an eco-place where curious, creative, and engaged people meet, all committed to living in greater harmony with nature.

## A Unique Rural Coliving Space

Located in the south of Aveyron, LAOM offers an exceptional setting: 21 hectares of forest, river, and meadows, where every space has been designed to foster creativity, connection, and well-being.

### Mountain Houses with Offices and Gardens

Our main space consists of mountain houses where each resident has a dedicated office and garden. Perfect for those seeking a balance between remote work and life in nature.

### A Rich and Diverse Community

At LAOM, you'll encounter dancers, permaculturists, eco-hackers, therapists, makers, coaches... This diversity creates a stimulating ecosystem where everyone learns from each other.

## Creative and Inspiring Spaces

- **Wooden desks** to work in a warm setting
- **Sunny sofas** for moments of relaxation
- **Tipis** and flower-filled nooks full of ideas
- **A shared kitchen** with homemade and open-source recipes

## Nature as a Partner

The Natural Park surrounding LAOM offers endless possibilities: hiking, running trails, cycling paths, tree climbing, or simply the pleasure of lying in the grass and watching the clouds.

## Join Us

Whether you want to stay for a few days, a few weeks, or settle in for a longer-term project, LAOM welcomes you. Each stay is an opportunity to recharge, create, and share.

**Contact us** to discover how LAOM can become your next space for living and creating.
